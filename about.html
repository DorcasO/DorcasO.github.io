About Dr Dorcas Ojo

Biologist | Data Scientist | Research Leader

Professional Background

With a PhD in Plant Genomics from the University of York and an MTech in Environmental Biology, I combine advanced biological knowledge with data science expertise to address complex challenges in plant breeding, food security, and healthcare outcomes. My research focuses on multi-omics approaches—integrating transcriptomics, metabolomics, and genomics—to understand biological systems at scale.

I am passionate about applied AI, research translation, and building ethical, reproducible, data-driven solutions that create real-world impact. My technical toolkit spans Python, R, SQL, machine learning, cloud platforms (AWS, Azure), and data visualization tools (Power BI, Tableau).
